Minion Endless Rush- By @yiosgio.

Minion Endless Rush is a game sort of like "Minion Rush". There are two missions available as of now in the demo: Boss Battle (Scarlet Overkill) from the Minions Movie or the Get Bananas Mission. Which requires you to collect a curtain amount of bananas before the mission is complete.
